Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

"The Espresso Room" Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

Analyzed data from historical sales data using Excel and its power features like power pivot, power query etc. I uncovered which location performs best, increased sales significantly, and identified top-performing and under-performing products.

Project Details

The Espresso Room Coffee Shop is a food and beverage chain, with over 3 shops, needed insights to analyze the impact of their sales on every location since its inception.

This project has unveiled some useful sales insights that could help the owners understand the market behavior, identify its trends and patterns, finding the Key Performance Indicators (KPI) that help “The Espresso Room” Coffee Shop business to unlock potential growth opportunities through the data-driven insights.

I created a "sales dashboard" view and built interactive dashboards to visualize trends, assess product performance, and measure promotion impact. This allowed for real-time analysis of sales and customer engagement.

The coffee shop's diverse product range and segmented pricing strategy have successfully catered to various customer needs with a consistent month-over-month growth of 16.27% across all locations.
